# ReliefCheck — Free Debt Relief Calculator

🔗 **[Try the Calculator →](https://reliefcheck.co)**

See how much you could save on your debt in 60 seconds. Compare top-rated debt relief companies with honest, independent reviews.

## Features
- 🧮 **Debt Calculator** — Enter your debt amount and see estimated savings instantly
- ⭐ **Company Reviews** — Honest, in-depth reviews of National Debt Relief, Freedom Debt Relief, Americor, and more
- 📊 **Side-by-Side Comparison** — Compare fees, ratings, and features of 7 top companies
- 🔒 **Free & Secure** — No credit check, no obligation, 256-bit SSL encryption

## How It Works
1. Enter your total debt amount
2. Select your debt types (credit cards, medical, personal loans, etc.)
3. Get your estimated savings in seconds
4. Compare top-rated companies
5. Get matched with a certified debt specialist
